Eric Goh is a researcher and entrepreneur with over eight years of experiences in multiple industries. His varied career includes data science research, intelligent system development, IT project management, and adjunct lecturer. He is the manager of boozedesign, and has recently founded SVBook and uploaded some of his software projects at LajuSoft. Eric holds a Master of Technology from NUS, an Executive MBA in IT Management from U21Global and IGNOU, and a Graduate Diploma in Mechatronics from A*STAR SIMTech. He is also a GSTF Certified Big Data Science Analyst, PRINCE2 Practitioner, AIIM Business Process Management Master, as well as an International Education Society Certified Lecturer.

Eric has also been a finalist in TelR Data Mining Competition for counterfeit banknotes prediction, a recipient of Tan Kah Kee Young Inventors’ Merit Award, and a 2nd RunnerUp in All Japan Micromouse Competition. He is a fellow at the Institute of Management Specialists, and has been inducted as full member into Sigma Xi and Upsilon Pi Epsilon Honor Societies for Scientific Research, Computing and Information Disciplines. He is the author of the book, “Learn By Examples – A Quick Guide to C# Programming”, published at LeanPub, which lias with his courses at EMHAcademy. His proficiencies includes SPSS Statistics and Modeler, SAS Enterprise Miner, R, Python, Tableau, Excel, Java, C#, C, SQL and etc. His hobbies include digital arts and photography, which can be found here.
